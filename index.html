Hearing Impaired in the Philippines

In the Philippines, 1.23% of the entire population is either deaf, mute, or hearing-impaired. As of 2009, the projected deaf population is already at 241,624 for those who are totally deaf, and 275,9J.2 for those who are partially deaf. That means that at least 517,536 people currently have very limited access to media and information because of their hearing impairedness.

AR Glasses Solution

AR in education allows students to gain knowledge through rich visuals and immersion into the subject matter. Moreover, speech technology also engages students by providing comprehensive details about the topic in a voice format. In short, the concept of eLearning with AR targets a major information-gathering sense in humans.

Speech-To-Text

Speech-to-text (STT) technology can be a valuable tool for hearing-impaired individuals in education by providing them with access to spoken information in a written format

ABOUT US

Are you hearing impaired?
Let EyeHearYou help!

EyeHearYou is a web application crafted to enhance inclusive education for individuals with hearing impairments. Leveraging augmented reality technology and a sophisticated speech-to-text system, the application seamlessly displays spoken content within a standard classroom setting.

By donning the provided glasses, users can log in to the application, facilitating clear and effective communication. This innovative solution serves as a sophisticated communication aid, ensuring a more inclusive and accessible educational experience for all participants.

OUR FEATURES

Explore Hearing Through Your Eyes

EyeHearYou uses a speech-to-text API as its main feature. Furthermore, it has a registration and login system, classroom and subject information, and its respective professors.

SPEECH-TO-TEXT API

A speech-to-text API is used in the application to gather speech and display them on the screen.

USER INFORMATION

A user can register and login so that they may have their personal informations set up within the class.

SUBJECT INFORMATION

EyeHearYou also displays the information about the subject and the class that the user currently has.
